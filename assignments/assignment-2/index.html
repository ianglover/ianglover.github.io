Assignment #2 - Typography on the Web

Typeface Specimen

Our homework assignment is to create a Typeface Specimen Page. Find a typeface that you like ( feel free to use the links and resources here or use your own ). We are going to create a Typeface Specimen Page demonstrating this fonts features, qualities, and information.

Be sure to include information on the Typeface's License if possible, but otherwise the design, color, and content of the presentation are completely up to you. You could also include information on its history or the foundry who created it.

Please be sure to demonstrate at least the following features of the font:

  • How the individual letters appear
  • How the font appears when used for words and complete phrases
  • Multiples sizes of the fonts
  • Multiple weights of the font
  • Multiple styles of the font
  • Some of the fonts Glyphs, see Intercode's Glyphs as an example
